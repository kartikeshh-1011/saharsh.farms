Discover Saharsh Farms

An Oasis of Tranquility Away From the City

Nestled amid coconut groves and organic mango plantations, Saharsh Farms offers the perfect staycation experience. Combining luxury contemporary lodging with the raw beauty of the countryside, our estate is designed to refresh your mind, body, and soul.

Whether you are planning a cozy weekend getaway with family, a private pool party with friends, or a peaceful corporate retreat, Saharsh Farms provides complete privacy, top-tier amenities, and unparalleled hospitality.

What We Offer

World-Class Amenities for Your Comfort

We provide all the premium comforts of a resort coupled with the privacy and warmth of a farmhouse.

Natural Plunge Pool

Indulge in our refreshing spring-fed natural water tank, filled with clean running stream water and surrounded by lush green coconut trees.

High-Speed Wi-Fi

Stay connected with high-speed internet throughout the farmhouse. Perfect for remote 'workations' amid nature.

Gazebo & Bonfire

Enjoy a starry night gathered around our warm stone firepit with barbecue setup, rustic seating, and fairy lights.

Indoor & Outdoor Games

Play badminton, cricket on the lawn, carrom, board games, or simply run free across our massive green meadows.

Kitchen & Private Chef

Equipped kitchen with refrigerator, microwave, and stove. You can request our local chef to prepare authentic regional meals.

Secure & Private

Fully gated property with 24/7 security staff, CCTV, ample car parking, and complete privacy for families and couples.
